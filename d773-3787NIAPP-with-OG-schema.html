Niagara Falls

Every Niagara Falls Power Pass also includes a colorful guide with maps, travel instructions, attraction descriptions and operating hours.

How it works:
The Niagara Falls Power Pass is valid for one, two, three or five consecutive days only. Present your Niagara Falls Power Pass at the ticket window of each attraction. Each participating location can be visited once, and your pass will be activated at the first participating location visited.

Please note:
The Niagara Falls Power Pass includes attractions on both the United States and Canadian sides of the Niagara Falls. Power Pass holders will need to provide the documentation required by each government to cross the borders. The Niagara Falls Power Pass cannot be used as a form of identification for border crossings.
