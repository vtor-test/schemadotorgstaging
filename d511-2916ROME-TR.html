Reviewed by David C, March 2013

We paid for the 48 hour ticket but spent so much time walking around Rome that we didn't get the most out of the ticket. On our first day we stayed on the bus for the full tour and that really helped us get our bearings and plan our route when walking to catch all the sights. The commentary was excellent.
