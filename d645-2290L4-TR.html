Reviewed by Marianne D, Australia, June 2012

Our night tour of Los Angeles was fantastic. Our driver/guide, Abel was great, knowledgable and hilarious! He made the tour. We saw all the places advertised and more.He knew his way around and drove really well. I would recommend this tour to everyone.

Reviewed by denise m, January 2013

We were only in town for a couple days. This was a great way to get to see many things in a short period of time. The driver was informative and entertaining. Wish we would have stopped on Rodeo Dr. and in Hollywood for a bit longer, but over all its is worth the money!

Report problem

Reviewed by victoria c, March 2013

the itinerary was fine, but our tour guide was lacking. Zero was friendly upon departure but lacked the ability to provide interesting or accurate information on the tour. He did not communicate clearly or effectively and just threw our random thoughts. He drove in some areas that were off the beaten path to avoid traffic and then paniced about being in residential areas at night without permission. This was more like having a taxi driver than a tour guide. I would not recommend this tour to others.
